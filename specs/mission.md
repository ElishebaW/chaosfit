# ChaosFit — Mission

## Mission Statement

Make effective fitness available to anyone, anywhere, regardless of time, space, or equipment constraints.

## North Star

A parent with 10 minutes and a 6x6 ft floor should get the same quality coaching as someone with a personal trainer.

## The Problem We Solve

Most fitness tooling is built for people who already have access — a gym membership, a dedicated hour, a spare room, a budget for equipment. ChaosFit is built for everyone else.

Real life is chaotic: babies cry mid-squat, meetings run over, the living room doubles as a workout space. The people who need fitness support most are the ones current solutions fail.

## Who We Build For

- **Parents with young children** — workouts that fit 12-minute nap windows and pause gracefully when interrupted
- **Busy professionals** — quick, efficient sessions that work in hotel rooms and between meetings
- **People in recovery** — adaptive, low-impact options that meet users where they are physically

## What "Success" Looks Like

A user opens ChaosFit, tells the coach what they have (5 minutes, a corner of their kitchen), and gets a safe, effective, personalized workout — with real-time form guidance — without needing a gym, equipment, or a scheduled appointment.

## What We Don't Do

- Store or train on user video data (privacy-first, real-time processing only)
- Require equipment, gym access, or dedicated workout space
- Penalize interruptions — session recovery is a first-class feature, not an afterthought
